The Bugatti Bolide is a track-only sports car developed by Bugatti
Engineering GmbH in Wolfsburg, Germany and Bugatti
Automobiles and manufactured in Molsheim, French automobile
manufacturer Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S.,
revealed online on October 28, 2020. According to Bugatti,
the concept version of the Bolide is using the W16 engine
with a weight-to-power-ratio of 0.91 kg/kW (0.67 kg/PS; 1.50 lb/hp).Bugatti announced
the Bolide would be the last car ever made with their 8.0 liter W16 engine with four turbochargers.
Bolide's name comes from the term le bolide, which literally means "the racing car" in French.
